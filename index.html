Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 27, 2025

This privacy policy applies to the iOS app Rollover.

Information Collection

Rollover does not collect or store any personally identifiable information. The app operates entirely on-device and does not require users to create an account or submit any personal data.

Third-Party Services

The app displays ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect anonymous usage data, such as ad impressions and click-through rates, in order to serve relevant ads and monitor performance. This data is collected and processed in accordance with Google's own privacy policies.

You can review Google’s privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Rollover is not specifically targeted at children under 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from users of any age.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please feel free to get in touch.
